Anahuac RV Park Rules & Regulations

1003 Davis Ave, Anahuac, TX 77514 • (409) 267-6603 • support@anrvpark.com

The following rules are designed to ensure all guests have a quiet, pleasant, and enjoyable stay… Please respect your neighbors and the park!
  1. Check out time is 1:00 pm for guests departing.
  2. Rent is due on time. Failure to pay on time will result in late fees or you will be asked to leave.
  3. A sewer hose seal (rubber donut or threaded connector) for your sewer hose is required for all sewer hook ups.
  4. All garbage must be placed in supplied dumpster in front of park. No garbage over 50lbs is allowed in dumpster. No large items protruding outside of dumpster allowed. Garbage bags and small items only.
  5. We offer no refunds for rent – we will refund remaining balance of security deposit after applying cost of electricity to last month of visit.
  6. Pets are welcome – only if pet lives indoors/inside the RV. All pets must be on a leash at all times and under physical control of owner/handler when outside. Pet owners are required to remove all of their pet feces immediately. All pets are to use green area when being walked on leash by owner. If owner cannot keep pet feces away from other sites, guest/visitor will be asked to leave. Please make sure your pets do not go after our ducks.
  7. Please do not leave anything poisonous outside (such as ant killer) or anything that would be harmful to the ducks.
  8. Children under the age of 18 – must be supervised at all times by parents. Please do not let your children chase or harm our ducks!
  9. Sites must be kept clean at all times. You may be charged a fee for any cleanup management has to do to your site in order to maintain it. If it continues, management may ask you to leave. Please help us to maintain grass/weeds around your site and personal property by keeping everything easily accessible for maintenance. This includes keeping all trash picked up around your site. All hoses/cords/personal belongings need to be off the ground or you are required to maintain the weeds around such items. We will not be responsible for any personal property that is damaged IF we have to cut down weeds around your belongings.
  10. No changes are allowed to sites including decks and or plants unless approved by management.
  11. No fires allowed except for pits or fire rings. No fireworks are allowed in the City limits. No smoking in the park buildings (bathroom, Washateria).
  12. No firearms or weapons allowed.
  13. The speed limit in the park is posted 5mph. Please respect the speed limit. There are children and ducks in the park. Be aware of your surroundings when driving. Anybody continuously not following the speed limit or reckless driving will be asked to leave.
  14. Washateria/Laundry Facility is unlocked 24hrs. But please insure you close the door well on your way out. If clothes are left unattended for longer than 30min., anybody has the right to remove the belongings to free up the machine. Please be mindful of your neighbors and do not keep a machine occupied for longer than needed. Use only American Made Quarters. Please no foreign objects in quarter slots.
  15. Quiet hours in RV Park and City Ordinance are from 10pm to 7am. No exceptions! Please keep all vehicle radios to a minimum while driving thru the park.
  16. No more than two people/cars are allowed per space. Rent is based on two people. An increase of $25 per additional person/vehicle/boat will be applied to rent if it is more than two.
  17. You are not allowed to share the wifi password with any visitors.
  18. Guests must park within their designated rented space only.
  19. Guests are only allowed two visitors at a time. Heavy traffic of visitors is not allowed. Guest will be held responsible for the actions of their visitors/family members.
  20. No subleasing allowed.
  21. Guests are not to violate the law in the use of the premises for immoral purposes, the sale of liquor, the sale or use of drugs, or any other illegal activities.
  22. Management reserves the right to immediately remove any guest or their visitors, who have shown a disregard for our rules, disrespect to management/property or other guests, without refund. Guest and their visitors agree to use the campground at their own risk. The management and owners are not responsible for damage, injury, or loss to persons, pets or property.
  23. All unauthorized parking will be towed away at vehicle/RV owners' expense and risk.
  24. We reserve the right to make changes to the rules, regulations and fees that we deem necessary.
EVERYONE IS CONSIDERED A GUEST IN THE PARK…. THERE IS NO TENANT/LANDLORD AGREEMENT
