⚠️ Severe Weather Tips for RV Residents tap to expand

🌀 Hurricane Warning Issued:

  • Follow all Chambers County evacuation orders immediately
  • Disconnect electric, water, and sewer hookups
  • Secure or remove awnings, antennas, and loose items
  • Take important documents, medications, and valuables with you
  • Do NOT stay in your RV during a hurricane

💨 High Wind Safety:

  • Retract all slide-outs and awnings
  • Lower antennas and satellite dishes
  • Chock wheels and set parking brake
  • Secure outdoor furniture, grills, and decorations
  • Keep stabilizer jacks deployed for stability

🚨 Emergency Contacts:

  • Park Office: (409) 267-6603
  • Chambers County Emergency: 911
  • Park Address: 1003 Davis Ave, Anahuac, TX 77514

Management will send SMS alerts for severe weather. Keep your phone charged and notifications on.

📋 Park Rules
  • Speed limit 5 MPH — children & ducks in park!
  • Quiet hours 10pm – 7am
  • Pets welcome on leash — clean up after them
  • Max 2 people per space ($25/extra person)
  • No open fires except fire pits/rings. No fireworks.
  • Keep your site clean at all times
  • Rent due the 1st — late fees after 3 days
  • No subleasing. No sharing WiFi password.
  • Max 2 visitors at a time
  • Management reserves the right to remove guests who violate rules
